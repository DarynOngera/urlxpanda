About URLXpanda

URLXpanda is a fast and secure URL expansion tool built with Rust and WebAssembly.

Features:

  • Secure client-side URL expansion
  • Support for all major URL shorteners
  • Configurable redirect limits and timeouts
  • No data sent to external servers
  • Fast WebAssembly performance

Privacy:

All URL expansion happens directly in your browser. No URLs are sent to our servers.
